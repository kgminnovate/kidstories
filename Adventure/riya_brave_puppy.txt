Title: The Brave Puppy  
Author: Riya  
Date: 2025-05-06  

Max was a tiny puppy, but he had a big heart.  
When his owner got lost in the woods, Max followed her scent through wind and rain.  
He barked loudly until a rescue team found them both.  
From that day, Max was known as the bravest puppy in the town.
